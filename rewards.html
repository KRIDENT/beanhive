How it works

Earning free drinks and food has never been easier. Here is how to get started in three simple steps.

Collect Stars

Earn 1 Star per dollar spent when you pay with cash, credit, or debit. Earn 2 Stars per dollar when you load funds onto your Beanhive card.

Enjoy free treats

Use your Stars to redeem free food, drinks, and more. The more Stars you collect, the bigger the rewards you can unlock.

Unlock benefits

Reach new tiers to unlock extras like free refills, birthday rewards, double-Star days, and exclusive member events.

Get rewarded for your routine

As you earn Stars, you will advance through tiers and unlock increasingly better benefits.

Green Status

Earn 100+ Stars each year

  • Free birthday drink or food item
  • Free in-store refills on brewed coffee and tea
  • Early access to new products
  • Personalized offers and discounts
  • Order ahead and skip the line

Gold Status

650+ Stars earned each year

  • Everything in Green Status
  • Monthly double-Star bonus days
  • Personalized Gold card
  • Free drink or food with every 125 Stars
  • Exclusive seasonal offers
  • Free drink size upgrades

Reserve Status

1,200+ Stars earned each year

  • Everything in Gold Status
  • Access to Reserve coffee selections
  • Invitations to exclusive tasting events
  • Free drink or food with every 100 Stars
  • Complimentary extra shots and syrups
  • Priority customer support
  • Annual surprise gift from Beanhive
